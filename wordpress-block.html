Will the excise cut reach the bowser?
From 1 April the government halved the fuel excise from 52.6c to 26.3c per litre — a saving of $13.15 on a 50L tank. Prices should drop within days as stations reprice stock. Forbes Australia is tracking whether retailers pass the full saving on. Check back daily.
